About the project

Career guidance made simple and practical

CareerPath Finder helps students and early professionals turn interests into realistic next steps.

Our Mission

CareerPath Finder is dedicated to helping people discover meaningful career paths that align with their passions, skills, and aspirations.

How It Works

The matching system analyzes your hobbies, dreams, goals, and skills to provide personalized career recommendations with resources to begin learning.
